To get started, use the navigation bar at the top of the page to select what attractions you would like to see; then click the update button.
In the bottom left corner, there is a text list of all attractions that your selection returned.
You will need to reset the selection before each search to clear all past selections.
If you want to zoom in on an attraction double click the marker on the map that corresponds to the attraction you would like to see close up.

If you use the "Max Price" selection and a very small amount of markers appear on the map, you may need to redo your search using the "Show All" option in "Max Price".
